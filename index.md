## Why UltimateSlice?

UltimateSlice is designed to bring a professional, FCP-like non-linear editing experience to the Linux desktop and beyond. Powered by **GStreamer** and written in **Rust**, it offers a solid foundation for both simple cuts and complex projects.

*   **Native Performance**: No Electron here. UltimateSlice is a true native application.
*   **Intuitive Timeline**: A familiar multi-track timeline with tools that just work.
*   **FCPXML Support**: Interchange projects with other professional editors.
*   **AI Integration**: Use the Model Context Protocol (MCP) to control your editor with AI.
