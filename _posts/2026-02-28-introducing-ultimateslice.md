Today marks a major milestone for the video editing community. We are incredibly excited to announce the release of **UltimateSlice v0.1.0**.

UltimateSlice is not just another video editor. It is a tool designed to bridge the gap between complex professional software and the ease-of-use that modern users expect.

### Why UltimateSlice?

1.  **Speed**: Built with performance in mind.
2.  **Design**: A UI that feels like it belongs on your favorite OS.
3.  **Community**: Open source and built for everyone.

Check out the [Features](/features/) page to see everything we've packed into this first release.

Stay tuned for more updates as we continue to build the future of video editing.
