# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to UltimateSlice! This guide will help you make your first edit in just five minutes.

### 1. Create a New Project

When you first launch UltimateSlice, you'll be greeted with the project manager. Click **New Project**, name your masterpiece, and choose your resolution and frame rate.

### 2. Import Your Media

You can drag and drop your video files, audio tracks, and images directly into the **Media Pool** on the left side of the screen.

### 3. Build Your Timeline

Drag your imported media from the Media Pool down into the **Timeline**. You can trim clips by dragging their edges, or use the **Blade Tool** (`B`) to split a clip in half.

### 4. Add Effects and Color

Select a clip in your timeline and navigate to the **Effects Inspector** to add transitions, text, or color correction. UltimateSlice comes with a suite of professional-grade presets.

### 5. Export Your Video

Once you're happy with your edit, click the **Export** button in the top right corner. Choose your format (MP4, ProRes, etc.) and destination, then click **Render**.

Congratulations! You've just created your first video with UltimateSlice.
